🌿 Berberine
AMPK Activator · Lipid + Glucose
750 mg/day
AMPK activation reducing LDL synthesis and improving insulin sensitivity. Key synergistic contributor to 56% LDL reduction alongside rosuvastatin. Beneficial for glucose at 5.8 mmol/L.
⚠ Sub-therapeutic — evidence base uses 900–1500 mg/day; consider titrating
✓ Minimal CYP3A4 concern with rosuvastatin specifically
✓ Tends to lower, not raise, liver enzymes
🐟 Krill Oil + Astaxanthin
Omega-3 EPA/DHA · Antioxidant
2000 mg/day
EPA/DHA in phospholipid form (high bioavailability) + trace astaxanthin. Triglycerides already 0.8 mmol/L; anti-inflammatory role is primary rationale at this dose.
⚠ Astaxanthin sub-therapeutic — µg vs 4–12 mg/day needed clinically
⚠ Small AF risk signal at sustained high omega-3 doses in CAD
✓ No statin interaction
🔬 NMN
NAD+ Precursor · Cellular Energy
500 mg/day
Raises intracellular NAD+. Promising early human data for vascular health, insulin sensitivity and arterial stiffness. TGA Listed in Australia from 10/12/2025.
⚠ At exact TGA maximum — do not exceed 500 mg/day
⚠ TGA recommends 12-week use cycles
⚠ Theoretical cancer concern (NAMPT) — unproven in humans
✓ No statin or cardiac interaction
💪 Creatine Monohydrate
Ergogenic · Strength · Cognitive
5 g/day — PLANNED
Strongest supplement evidence for resistance training in men 50+. Supports phosphocreatine-ATP system. Modest cognitive benefit. No loading phase needed.
⚠ MUST get cystatin-C eGFR baseline first — raises creatinine artifactually
✓ No CYP3A4 interaction · May blunt exercise-CK (beneficial given CK 330)
✓ No cardiovascular or myocardial bridge contraindication
